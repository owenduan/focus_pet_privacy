🥚 蛋蛋专注 — 隐私政策

中文版本 · 最后更新日期：2026 年 6 月 3 日

1. 引言

欢迎使用「蛋蛋专注」（以下简称"本应用"或"我们"）。我们深知个人信息对您的重要性，并会尽全力保护您的个人信息安全可靠。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储和共享您的信息。

2. 我们收集的信息

2.1 您主动提供的信息

  • 宠物昵称：当您为孵化的宠物取昵称时，昵称文本将保存在您的设备本地存储中。

2.2 自动收集的信息

本应用不会自动收集任何个人身份信息。以下数据仅存储在您的设备本地（通过 Hive 数据库）：

  • 专注计时记录（专注时长、开始/结束时间、完成状态）
  • 宠物孵化记录（宠物类型、孵化时间）
  • 用户统计数据（累计专注分钟数、连续专注天数）

3. 信息的使用

我们使用上述信息仅用于以下目的：

  • 在应用内展示您的专注历史、宠物图鉴和统计数据
  • 计算和显示您的连续专注天数
  • 提供个性化的宠物孵化体验

4. 信息的存储

  • 本地存储：所有数据均存储在您的设备本地（Hive 轻量数据库）。我们不会将您的数据上传至任何远程服务器。
  • 数据安全：您的数据保留在您的设备上，由您完全掌控。卸载应用将永久删除所有本地数据。

5. 信息的共享与披露

我们不会向任何第三方出售、交易或转让您的个人信息。我们不会与任何公司、组织或个人共享您的数据，因为所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地。

6. 第三方服务

本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

7. 儿童隐私

本应用面向所有年龄段的用户。我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您是家长或监护人，发现您的孩子在未经您同意的情况下向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们。

8. 您的权利

由于所有数据均存储在您的设备本地，您可以随时：

  • 卸载应用以永久删除所有数据
  • 在应用内查看您的所有数据（专注记录、宠物图鉴、统计数据）

9. 隐私政策的变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本将在应用更新时生效。我们建议您定期查看本页面以了解任何变更。

10. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

  • GitHub Issues：https://github.com/owenduan/focus_pet/issues

🥚 Focus Pet — Privacy Policy

English Version · Last updated: June 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Focus Pet ("the App", "we", "us" or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and share your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Pet Nicknames: When you name a hatched pet, the nickname text is stored locally on your device.

2.2 Automatically Stored Information

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. The following data is stored exclusively on your device (via Hive database):

  • Focus session records (duration, start/end time, completion status)
  • Pet hatching records (pet type, hatch time)
  • User statistics (total focus minutes, consecutive focus days)

3. How We Use Information

We use the above information solely for:

  • Displaying your focus history, pet collection, and statistics within the App
  • Calculating and displaying your focus streak
  • Providing a personalized pet hatching experience

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using the Hive lightweight database. No data is uploaded to any remote server.
  • Data Security: Your data remains on your device under your full control. Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all local data.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party. We do not share your data with any company, organization, or individual, as all data is stored solely on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you may at any time:

  • Uninstall the App to permanently delete all data
  • View all your data within the App (focus records, pet collection, statistics)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will take effect when the App is updated. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via:

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/owenduan/focus_pet/issues
